Phase 1: The Daily Check-In (Data Collection)
The inputs need to change based on where she is in her cycle.

A. The "Vitals" (The Flow & Fluid)
Bleeding Status:

Options: None / Spotting (Brown/Pink) / Light / Medium / Heavy (Flooding) / Clots (Size of a coin? Size of a golf ball?).

Why: "Flooding" (changing protection every hour) is a medical red flag (Menorrhagia).

Cervical Mucus (The Hidden Biomarker):

Options: Dry / Sticky / Creamy / Egg-white (Stretchy & Clear).

Why: This tells you biologically if she is fertile right now, more accurately than just counting days.

B. The "Pain" Profile (Dysmenorrhea)
Pain Location:

Options: Lower Belly / Lower Back / Thighs / Headache / Breasts.

Pain Intensity (1-10):

Clinical Threshold: If pain is >7 and not relieved by ibuprofen, this is a major red flag for Endometriosis.

C. The "Hormonal" Symptoms
Digestion: "Bloating, constipation, or diarrhea ('Period Poop')?"

Why: Prostaglandins cause the uterus to contract but also affect the gut.

Skin: "Greasy, dry, or breaking out?" (Chin/Jawline = Hormonal).

Libido (Sex Drive): High / Low / Neutral.

Why: Spikes during ovulation (Nature wanting you to reproduce).

Phase 2: AI Logic & Triage (The "Brain")
The AI must understand the 4 Seasons of the Cycle. It shouldn't just say "Day 12"; it should interpret the biology.

Shutterstock

❄️ Phase 1: Menstruation (The "Winter") - Days 1-5
Biology: Estrogen and Progesterone are at rock bottom. The lining is shedding.

User Status: Low energy, cramping, bleeding.

AI Suggestion: "Your hormones are at their lowest. It's okay to cancel plans."

Diet: "Eat warm, iron-rich foods (spinach, red meat) to replace blood loss. Avoid caffeine (it makes cramps worse)."

Exercise: "Gentle stretching or Yoga. No HIIT today."

🌱 Phase 2: The Follicular Phase (The "Spring") - Days 6-11
Biology: Estrogen is rising rapidly. FSH (Follicle Stimulating Hormone) is working.

User Status: Energy returns, brain fog clears, skin glows.

AI Suggestion: "You are entering your power window."

Action: "This is the best time to tackle hard projects at work or try a personal best in the gym."

☀️ Phase 3: Ovulation (The "Summer") - Days 12-16
Biology: Estrogen peaks. Luteinizing Hormone (LH) surges to release the egg.

User Status: Peak libido, magnetic energy, fertile mucus (Egg-white).

AI Suggestion: "You are at peak fertility."

If 'Trying to Conceive' mode: "Go for it! Highest probability today/tomorrow."

If 'Avoiding Pregnancy' mode: "Use protection! Sperm can survive for 5 days waiting for this egg."

Symptom Watch: "Do you feel a sharp twinge on one side? That's Mittelschmerz (ovulation pain). It's normal."

🍂 Phase 4: The Luteal Phase (The "Autumn") - Days 17-28
Biology: Progesterone dominates. The body prepares for a possible baby. If no baby, hormones crash at the end.

User Status: PMS zone. Bloating, breast tenderness, moodiness, cravings.

AI Suggestion: "Progesterone is high, which naturally slows you down."

Diet: "Craving carbs? That's normal. Try complex carbs (oats/sweet potato) to stabilize serotonin."

Mental: "You might feel more critical or introverted. Don't make big life decisions (like breaking up) this week."

Phase 3: Specialized "Red Flag" Detection
This is where your app saves lives or fertility.

1. The "Endo" Detector (Endometriosis)
Logic: If user consistently logs Pain > 8, Pain during sex, or Pain during bowel movements.

AI Alert: "Your pain patterns are outside the 'normal' range. Severe cramps that stop you from working are NOT something you just have to 'live with.' Please show this log to a specialist to screen for Endometriosis."

2. The "PCOS" Pattern (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome)
Logic:

Cycles > 35 days (Oligomenorrhea).

User logs "Acne" + "Chin Hair" (Hirsutism) + "Weight Gain."

No clear temperature rise (indicating Anovulation - no egg released).

AI Alert: "You are having very long cycles and signs of high androgens (male hormones). This pattern looks like PCOS. Ask your doctor for a blood panel (Testosterone/LH/FSH)."

3. The "Spotting" Check (Polyps/Fibroids)
Logic: User logs bleeding between periods (Intermenstrual bleeding).

AI Alert: "You are bleeding outside your window. This can be caused by a polyp, a fibroid, or an infection. This requires a physical exam."

Phase 4: Pattern Recognition (The Engineer's Edge)
Cycle Syncing (The "Wellness" Angle):

Concept: Matching lifestyle to hormones.

Feature: The app generates a "Grocery List" and "Workout Plan" based on the phase.

Example (Luteal Phase): "Add Magnesium and B6 to your cart (helps PMS). Switch gym routine to Pilates."

The "Late" Panic Calculator:

Scenario: Period is 3 days late. User is panicking.

AI Logic: Check past data. "Did you log 'High Stress' or 'Illness' or 'Travel' (jet lag) two weeks ago (during ovulation)?"

AI Insight: "Stress delays ovulation, which delays the period. Since you were sick on the 14th, your period is likely just delayed, not missing. Take a test on the 5th if it hasn't arrived."

Discharge Decoding:

Pattern: If user logs "Thick, white, clumpy discharge" + "Itching."

AI Diagnosis: Likely Yeast Infection (Candida).

Pattern: "Grey, watery discharge" + "Fishy smell."

AI Diagnosis: Likely Bacterial Vaginosis (BV).

Suggestion: "This looks like an infection, not a cycle symptom. Visit a clinic."

Summary for the App Developer
The "Normal" is a Range: A 28-day cycle is a myth for many. 21-35 days is the medical "normal."

Privacy: This data is extremely sensitive (sexual activity, pregnancy attempts).

Terminology: Use "Menstrual Products" (Pads, Tampons, Cups, Discs) to be inclusive.

Visuals: Use a Circle/Wheel Interface for the main dashboard. It is the most intuitive way to visualize a cycle.
